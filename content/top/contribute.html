How to contribute to

Workerpedia is an open-source search engine for responses to misinformation and solutions to worker problems.

Consider what information you can provide that would be most useful to average workers and/or workers looking for effective responses to popular talking points from special interests or the alt-right.

Your entry might be

An article showing how to reply to information on social media you know to be false, with links to sources of evidence.

For Example: What are some recent challenging conversations you've had and how did you respond?

A how to guide about something that will make everyday life better for workers, but may not be popular on other sources.

For Example: How could somebody get involved with citizen groups or unions in your city or region?

A response and trusted sources for a trending news item as breaking news often carries rampant speculation and misinformation.

For Example: The rise in hate crime as a result of COVID-19 makes urgent, accurate info a necessity.
